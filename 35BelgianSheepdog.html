Belgian Sheepdog

The Belgian Sheepdog, also known as the Belgian Groenendael, is a medium to large herding dog breed that originated in Belgium. It was originally bred to herd sheep and guard farms, and it is one of the four Belgian Shepherd varieties. This breed is known for its intelligence, agility, and strong protective instincts.

Belgian Sheepdogs have a long, thick, and straight black coat that gives them a very elegant and majestic appearance. Their fur is dense and weather-resistant, which helps protect them in cold and harsh environments. They have a proud posture, erect ears, and a confident expression that reflects their alert nature.
