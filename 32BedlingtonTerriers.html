Bedlington Terriers

The Bedlington Terrier is a small to medium-sized dog breed that originated in the mining towns of Northumberland and Durham in England. Initially bred as a hunting and vermin-control dog, Bedlington Terriers were used to hunt rabbits and other small game. Despite their working background, they have a gentle and affectionate nature that makes them excellent family companions.
