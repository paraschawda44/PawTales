American Water Spaniel

The American Water Spaniel is a medium-sized sporting dog breed that originated in the United States. It was developed to be a versatile hunting dog that could work both on land and in water, especially for retrieving birds and game. This breed is one of the few dog breeds that were developed entirely in America.

American Water Spaniels have a strong, muscular body and a curly or wavy coat that is water-resistant. Their coat color is usually liver, brown, or dark chocolate. The thick coat helps protect them from cold water, but it also requires regular brushing and grooming to prevent matting and keep it healthy.

In personality, American Water Spaniels are friendly, intelligent, and eager to please. They are very loyal to their family and enjoy being around people. They are also known for being gentle with children and friendly with other pets, especially when properly socialized.

This breed is energetic and active, so it needs daily exercise, training, and outdoor activities. They love swimming, running, and playing games. Because they are smart and hardworking, they enjoy learning new tricks and commands. With proper care, grooming, and training, the American Water Spaniel becomes a loving, energetic, and reliable companion dog.
