Belgian Tervuren

The Belgian Tervuren is a medium to large herding dog breed that originated in Belgium. It is one of the four Belgian Shepherd varieties and was originally bred to herd sheep and guard farms. This breed is known for its intelligence, agility, and strong work ethic.

Belgian Tervurens have a beautiful long, thick, and straight coat that is usually fawn to mahogany in color with a black overlay and black mask on the face. Their elegant appearance, alert ears, and confident posture make them look very noble and intelligent. Their coat also protects them from cold and harsh weather conditions.

In personality, the Belgian Tervuren is loyal, alert, and very devoted to its family. These dogs are highly protective and make excellent watchdogs and guard dogs. They are affectionate with their owners but can be reserved with strangers, which makes early socialization very important.
